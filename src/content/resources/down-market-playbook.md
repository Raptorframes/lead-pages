## What's Inside

Markets crashing. Wars escalating. And the best fund manager in the market has his **lowest trading activity in years**. This playbook explains why doing nothing is often the winning strategy, how to tell the difference between volatility (price noise) and real risk (business deterioration), and a step-by-step framework for making decisions when your instincts scream "sell everything."

## Who This Is For

Investors feeling the urge to panic-sell during geopolitical uncertainty, or anyone who wants a calm, data-driven framework for navigating volatile markets without destroying their returns.
