## What's Inside

This guide walks you through the complete RESP setup — from opening the account to picking the right ETF. You'll see the **exact $208/month contribution strategy** that maximizes your Canada Education Savings Grant, the **catch-up formula** if you've missed previous years, and a **compound growth table** showing what your money becomes over 5, 10, 14, and 18 years.

It also covers the **low-income bonus grants** most families don't know about — an extra 10-20% on top of the standard CESG for families under $55,867 in net income.

## Who This Is For

This is for Canadian parents who want to build real wealth for their children's education — whether you're starting from day one with a newborn or catching up with a 10-year-old. If you've been putting off opening an RESP because it felt complicated, this guide makes it simple: one account, one auto-deposit, one ETF, and the government does the rest.
