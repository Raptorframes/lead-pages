## What's Inside

Everyone says delay your CPP. But the **breakeven age for 60 vs 65 is 74** — and 35% of Canadian men don't make it to 75. This guide shows you the real math, the mortality stats from Statistics Canada, and a framework for deciding when to take CPP.

## Who This Is For

Canadians approaching 60 who are deciding when to start CPP, or anyone helping their parents make this decision.
