## What's Inside

The research is brutal: **70% of wealthy families lose everything by generation 2**, and 90% by the third. The cause is almost never bad investments — it's **lack of communication**. This checklist gives you the 5 steps that separate families who preserve wealth from those who don't.

## Who This Is For

Canadian parents and grandparents who have built wealth and want to make sure it survives the next generation — whether that's $100K or $10M.
